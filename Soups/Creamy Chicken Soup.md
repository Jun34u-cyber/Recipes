This soup will cure all of your ills...
## Prep
### Ingredients
*This includes ingredients for [[Chicken Bone Broth]], which is a part of this recipe.
* 8 Large Carrots
* 1 bunch of Kale
* 2 small gold potatoes
* 3 large thumbs of Ginger
* 2 Leeks
* 3 Garlic Bulb
* 2 Onion
* 4 Jalapenos
* 1 Rotisserie Chicken
* 1 block Silken Tofu
* 1/3 cup lentils
* 1/2 cup brown rice
* lime juice
* Curry powder
* Miso paste

1) Preheat oven to 400
2) Clean leeks
	1) Chop Leeks in half and separate
	2) Wash thoroughly and dry
3) Half potatoes
4) Chop onion in half, leaving skin on
5) Chop Garlic in half

#### Use the following ingredients to make [[Chicken Bone Broth]]:
* 4 Large Carrots
* 2 small gold potatoes
* 2 large thumbs of Ginger
* 1 Leek
* 1 Garlic Bulb
* 1 Onion
* 1 Rotisserie Chicken
	* Set aside chicken meat to shred

While the bone broth is cooking:
* shred chicken
#### Soup Prep
1) Chop remaining vegetables into large pieces:
	* Leek
	* 2 small onions
	* 2 heads of garlic (after peeling)
	* 4 carrots
	* 3 cups Kale
2) Grate remaining thumb of ginger
3) Slice jalapeno into thin rounds

## To Make
#### Soup Base
1) Sautee in a large pot:
	* Leek
	* Onion
	* Garlic
	* Carrot
	* Grated Ginger
	* Curry Powder
2) Once the veggies have cooked down a bit, add the following, and stir:
	* Brown rice
	* lentils
3) Pour in ~8 cups of broth
4) Let simmer on LOW for 45 min - 1 hour (mostly covered)
5) Remove 2 cups of vegetables from the pot and set aside
6) Stir in shredded chicken and simmer on LOW
#### Cream
1) Place 2 cups of cooked vegetables into a saucepan
2) Add 3 cups of Kale and cook
3) Blend with the block of silken tofu
### Final Soup
1) Return "cream" to the pot and mix in well
2) Mix in 2 TBSP of miso paste
3) Season to taste with the following
	* Salt
	* Pepper
	* Lime Juice
	* Curry Powder
