## Prep
### Ingredients
* 4 Large Carrots
* 2 small gold potatoes
* 2 large thumbs of Ginger
* 1 Leek
* 1 Garlic Bulb
* 1 Onion
* 1 Rotisserie Chicken

1) Preheat oven to 400
2) Clean leek
	1) Chop Leek in half and seprate
	2) Wash thoroughly and dry
2) Half potatoes
3) Chop onion in half, leaving skin on
4) Chop Garlic in half

## To Make:
1) Lay veggies on a greased roasting tray
	1) Sprinkle with olive oil, salt, and pepper
	2) Bake veggies at 400 for 15 minutes
	3) Broil for 2 minutes
2) Strip meat off of the rotisserie chicken with your bare hands like the feral creature that you are
	* Snack on some
	* Store the rest - shred it for soup.
3) To the Instant Pot, add:
	* Bird Carcass
	* Scorched veggies
	* 8 cups of water
	* Salt, pepper, herbs, and spices
		* I used Italian spices in a tea bag
4) Pressure cook on high pressure for 1 hour
	* Let instant pot "natural pressure release" for 30 min
5) Strain and enjoy
